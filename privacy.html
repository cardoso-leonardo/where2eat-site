Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you sign in with Google, the name and profile photo URL provided by your Google account. We store the display name, username, and avatar emoji you choose in your profile — the app does not support uploading photos, so we do not store any profile photos you take or select yourself. We also store the restaurant lists, ratings, comments, and visited-place marks you create, along with the lists other people invite you to.

Location

If you allow it, we use your device's location only while the app is open to show nearby restaurants first and to power the random-pick feature. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings — the app still works without it.

Third-party services

We use Firebase (Google) for sign-in and for storing your account and list data; Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple for authentication; and Google Maps/Places to search for and display restaurants. These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy terms. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or ad tracking.

Sharing with other users

People you invite to a shared list can see your display name, username, avatar, and the ratings/comments you post in that list. List owners and admins can remove a member from a list, and any member can report or block another member — see the members list in a shared list for these options.

Your choices

You can edit or delete your profile information at any time from the Profile screen. Deleting your account (Profile → Delete account) permanently removes your profile and rating history, and removes any lists you own for everyone. This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email where2eat.help@gmail.com.
