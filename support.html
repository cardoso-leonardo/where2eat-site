Contact us

Questions, bug reports, or feedback? Email where2eat.help@gmail.com — include your device type and app version if you're reporting a problem, so we can track it down faster.

Do I need an account?

No. Tap "Continue as guest" to create lists, search restaurants, rate spots, and use the random picker with no sign-up. Create an account with email, Google, or Sign in with Apple when you're ready to invite friends to a list.

How do I join a friend's list?

Ask the list owner or a member to share their invite code from the list's members screen, then enter it from the home screen to join.

How do I report or block someone?

Open the members list on any shared list, or a restaurant's comments, and use the report or block option next to their name. Blocked members can no longer see your activity or contact you. See the members list in a shared list for these options.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile and rating history, and removes any lists you own for everyone.

Something's not working

Try closing and reopening the app first. If the problem sticks around, email us at where2eat.help@gmail.com with a short description of what happened — screenshots help a lot.
